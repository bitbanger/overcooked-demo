What is VAL?

VAL is an intelligent, bidirectional tutoring system. Those directions are learning tasks from an expert, and teaching tasks to a student. VAL helps scale up tutoring by learning something from you once, and then explaining it to many students.

VAL combines ChatGPT with explainable AI to produce general, correct explanations without hallucinating.
